Mariah Carey

“Mariah Carey, otherwise known as “Songbird Supreme”, was born on March 27, 1969) She is a multi-talented powerful melodic singer and songwriter. She has also self-produced her own music and graced us with her beauty on the TV/Film screen. Mariah Carey is credited for impacting the vocal style in contemporary music, merging hip-hop with pop music through her collaborations and popularizing the use of remixes" (Wikipedia, 2022)
