Capabilities

Communications Intelligence

We have expertise in data acquisition, protocol processing, database and storage, analysis and presentation of large volumes of complex data for lawful interception and communications intelligence.

Supply Chain Management

We manage the flow of goods and services. It includes the movement and storage of raw materials, work-in-process inventory, and finished goods from point of origin to point of consumption.

Detection System

We create solutions that detect intrusions and intrusion attempts with patented software solutions designed to monitor your network and identify potential threats to your data.

Information Assurances

Measures that protect and defend information and information systems by ensuring their availability, integrity, authentication, confidentiality, and non-repudiation.

Systems Architecture

Our architecture is a conceptual model that defines the structure, behavior, and more views of a system. We create a formal description and representation of a system, organized in a way that supports reasoning about the structures and behaviors of the system.

Software Intergration


Our engineers apply the principles of software engineering to the design, development, maintenance, testing, and evaluation of the software and systems that make your hardware or and systems work effectively.
